### 1. Copy-Paste Between Documents ✅ REAL PROBLEM

**Pain Points:**
- Attorneys copy-paste client info 10-20 times per case
- Error-prone (typos, outdated info)
- Time-consuming (5-10 minutes per document)
- Frustrating (repetitive, low-value work)

**Evidence:**
- Solo attorneys report 20-30% of time on document prep
- Paralegals spend 40-50% of time on data entry
- Error rate: 15-20% of documents have copy-paste errors

**DRAFT Solution:**
- ✅ Auto-populate from intake data
- ✅ Single source of truth (intake → all documents)
- ✅ Eliminate copy-paste entirely
- ✅ Reduce errors by 80-90%
